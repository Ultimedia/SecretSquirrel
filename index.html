We strongly believe it’s important to understand people first before actually designing or building the website...

If you’re guessing, you can have it wrong

We aim to understand our users first by doing research, before actually designing and building something, which might otherwise be based on guesses and which often turns out to be wrong. This process is called user-centred design.

Qualitative research methodes

We aim to get and idea of people’s behaviours and habits so we can truly design the right tool for them. We use diary studies, in depth interviews, observations, workshops and so on to achieve this.
